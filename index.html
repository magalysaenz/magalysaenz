I am a first year Econ PhD Student at the University of Delaware.

I am interested in development economics, especially with it's intersections with public economics, labor markets, gender and rural development.

I was researching how reforms regarding withholding instruments and third party reporting impact tax compliance and revenue. We have a Working Paper where we look at Electronic Payment Technology and Tax Compliance exploiting different instruments of a Financial Inclusion Reform in Uruguay.

I hold a master in Applied Economics from the University of Massachusetts Boston and a Bachelor in Economics from Universidad Thomas More in Nicaragua, my home country.
